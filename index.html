KẾ HOẠCH HỌC TIẾNG ANH 8 TUẦN

Dành cho trẻ em 11 tuổi (lớp 6)

8 tuần học

16 bài đọc

Học thuộc lòng

Mục tiêu chương trình

  • Rèn luyện mỗi tuần 2 bài đọc (~100 từ/bài)
  • Tập trung vào chủ đề quen thuộc, từ vựng thông dụng
  • Cải thiện khả năng đọc - ghi nhớ - phát âm
  • Tăng tự tin giao tiếp tiếng Anh cơ bản

Lịch học hàng tuần

Ngày 1-3

Bài 1: Học từ vựng, luyện phát âm, ghi nhớ

Ngày 4-6

Bài 2: Tương tự bài 1

Ngày 7

Ôn tập cả 2 bài, kể lại không nhìn giấy

TUẦN 1: GIỚI THIỆU BẢN THÂN

Bài 1: About Myself

Hello! My name is Tom. I am eleven years old. I live in Vietnam with my family. I have one sister and one brother. My sister's name is Linh. She is thirteen years old. My brother is Nam. He is nine years old. We live in a small house near the school. I like to play soccer with my friends after school. My favorite color is blue. I also like to read books and draw pictures. My family has a small dog named Lucky. Lucky is very friendly and loves to play. Every morning, I wake up at six o'clock and help my mother in the kitchen. I am excited to learn English and make new friends. Learning English is fun and important for my future. I want to speak English well.

Từ vựng chủ chốt:

Bài 2: My Daily Routine

Every day, I wake up at six thirty in the morning. First, I brush my teeth and wash my face. Then I help my mother prepare breakfast for the family. We usually eat rice, eggs, and drink milk. After breakfast, I put on my school uniform and walk to school with my friends. School starts at seven thirty. I have six classes every day: math, Vietnamese, English, science, history, and art. My favorite subject is English because it is interesting. At twelve o'clock, we have lunch at school. I eat with my classmates and we talk about our lessons. School finishes at four thirty. After school, I play with my friends for one hour. Then I go home and do my homework. In the evening, I help my parents with housework. We have dinner together at seven o'clock. Before going to bed, I watch TV or read a book.

Từ vựng chủ chốt:

TUẦN 2: GIA ĐÌNH

Bài 3: My Wonderful Family

I love my family very much. My father is a teacher at the local school. He is forty years old and very kind. He helps me with my homework every evening. My mother is a nurse at the hospital. She is thirty-eight years old and works very hard. She takes care of our family and cooks delicious meals. My grandmother lives with us. She is seventy years old but still very active. She tells us interesting stories about the past. My grandfather passed away last year, but we still remember him. On weekends, our family likes to visit the market together. We buy fresh vegetables, fruits, and fish. Sometimes we go to the park and have a picnic. My family always supports me in everything I do. They encourage me to study hard and be a good person. I am grateful to have such a loving family.

Bài 4: Family Activities

My family enjoys doing many activities together. On Saturday mornings, we clean the house together. My father sweeps the yard while my mother washes the dishes. My sister and I organize our rooms and help with laundry. After cleaning, we often go shopping at the supermarket. We buy food for the whole week. On Sunday afternoons, we visit my uncle's family. They live near the river, and we like to go fishing together. My cousins and I play games while the adults talk. Sometimes we have barbecue parties in their garden. During holidays, our extended family gathers for big celebrations. My aunts cook traditional dishes, and we share stories and laugh together. These family moments are very precious to me. They teach me the importance of love, cooperation, and respect. I hope our family will always stay close and happy like this.

TUẦN 3: TRƯỜNG HỌC

Bài 5: My School

My school is called Nguyen Trai Primary School. It is a beautiful building with two floors and twenty classrooms. The school has a big playground where students play during break time. There is also a library with many interesting books. I love spending time there reading stories and learning new things. Our school has a computer room with twenty computers. We learn how to use computers and the internet. The teachers at my school are very friendly and helpful. My homeroom teacher is Mrs. Hoa. She teaches us Vietnamese and math. She is patient and always encourages us to do our best. The school cafeteria serves healthy meals every day. They have rice, meat, vegetables, and soup. After lunch, we have a short rest before afternoon classes. My school also has a beautiful garden with many flowers and trees. Students help water the plants and keep the garden clean.

Bài 6: My Classmates and Friends

I have many good friends at school. My best friend is Minh. He sits next to me in class. Minh is very smart and always helps me with difficult math problems. He likes to play basketball during recess. Another good friend is Lan. She is the best student in our class. Lan is good at English and often helps other students practice pronunciation. She wants to become a doctor when she grows up. There is also Duc, who is very funny and makes everyone laugh. He loves to tell jokes and stories. Our class has thirty students, and we all get along well. We work together on group projects and help each other with homework. During sports time, we play soccer, volleyball, and badminton. Sometimes we have class parties to celebrate birthdays and special occasions. These friendships make school life more enjoyable and meaningful. I am lucky to have such wonderful classmates and friends.

TUẦN 4: SỞ THÍCH & HOẠT ĐỘNG

Bài 7: My Hobbies

I have several hobbies that I enjoy doing in my free time. My favorite hobby is reading books. I love adventure stories and tales about brave heroes. Reading helps me learn new words and improves my imagination. I also like to draw and paint. I often draw pictures of my family, friends, and pets. My mother bought me a set of colored pencils for my birthday. Another hobby I enjoy is playing soccer. Every afternoon after school, I play with my friends at the local park. Soccer helps me stay healthy and strong. I also collect stamps from different countries. My uncle travels a lot and brings me stamps from his trips. I have stamps from Japan, Thailand, and Singapore. On rainy days, I like to listen to music and play simple songs on my guitar. My father taught me how to play basic chords. These hobbies make me happy and help me relax after studying.

Bài 8: Weekend Activities

Weekends are my favorite time of the week. On Saturday morning, I usually wake up late, around eight o'clock. After breakfast, I help my parents with housework. I sweep the floor and organize my room. Then I have free time to do what I like. Sometimes I visit my grandparents who live nearby. My grandmother always prepares delicious snacks for me. We sit together and she tells me stories about her childhood. On Saturday afternoon, I often go to the park with my friends. We play games, ride bicycles, and have fun together. Sunday is usually a family day. We go to the market to buy fresh food, then we cook together. My favorite Sunday activity is going to the bookstore with my father. We look at new books and I choose one to read. In the evening, we watch movies or TV shows as a family. Weekends help me rest and recharge for the new school week.

TUẦN 5: THỨC ĂN & ĐỒ UỐNG

Bài 9: My Favorite Foods

I love Vietnamese food very much. My favorite dish is pho, a delicious noodle soup with beef or chicken. My mother makes the best pho in our neighborhood. She cooks the broth for many hours to make it tasty. I also like banh mi, a Vietnamese sandwich with meat, vegetables, and sauce. For breakfast, I usually eat sticky rice with mung beans or peanuts. Sometimes I have bread with butter and jam. At lunch, I prefer rice with grilled fish, vegetables, and soup. My favorite fruit is mango because it is sweet and juicy. I also like bananas, apples, and oranges. For snacks, I enjoy eating crackers, cookies, and sometimes ice cream. When I visit my friends, their mothers often give me different foods to try. I have tasted Chinese noodles, Japanese sushi, and Korean kimchi. All foods are interesting, but I still love Vietnamese cuisine the most. Good food makes me happy and gives me energy to study and play.

Bài 10: Cooking with Family

Every Sunday, my family cooks together. It is one of our favorite traditions. My mother is the main cook, but everyone helps. My father is good at cutting vegetables and preparing meat. My sister helps wash the rice and vegetables. I usually help set the table and carry dishes. First, we plan what to cook for the week. We look at recipes and decide on the menu. Then we go shopping for ingredients at the local market. We buy fresh vegetables, meat, fish, and spices. When we come home, we start cooking together. My mother teaches us how to make different dishes. Last week, we learned to make spring rolls. It was fun wrapping the rice paper with vegetables and shrimp. We also made fried rice with eggs and vegetables. While cooking, we talk and laugh together. The kitchen becomes full of delicious smells. When the food is ready, we eat together and share stories about our week. Cooking together makes our family closer and teaches me valuable life skills.

TUẦN 6: ĐỘNG VẬT & THIÊN NHIÊN

Bài 11: Animals I Love

I love animals very much. At home, we have a small dog named Lucky. Lucky is brown and white with long ears and a short tail. He is very friendly and loves to play with children. Every morning, Lucky wakes me up by licking my face. He follows me everywhere I go. We also have two cats, Mimi and Coco. They are sisters and look very similar. They like to sleep in sunny spots and chase butterflies in the garden. My favorite wild animals are elephants and tigers. I think elephants are very intelligent and gentle. They take care of their families just like humans do. Tigers are beautiful but dangerous. They are strong and fast hunters. At the zoo, I also like to see monkeys, giraffes, and colorful birds. Monkeys are funny and playful. Giraffes are very tall and eat leaves from high trees. Birds can fly and sing beautiful songs. I believe all animals deserve our love and protection. We should take care of them and their homes in nature.

Bài 12: Nature Around Us

Nature is everywhere around us and it is very beautiful. In my neighborhood, there are many tall trees that provide shade and fresh air. The trees have green leaves and sometimes colorful flowers. Birds make their nests in the branches and sing lovely songs. In spring, new flowers bloom in gardens and parks. I love to see red roses, yellow sunflowers, and purple orchids. The flowers smell wonderful and attract bees and butterflies. Near my house, there is a small pond with clear water. Fish swim in the pond and frogs jump on the lily pads. At night, I can hear crickets singing and see fireflies glowing in the dark. During summer, it often rains and everything becomes fresh and green. The air smells clean after the rain. Mountains in the distance look blue and peaceful. Rivers flow quietly through the countryside. All these natural things make me feel happy and calm. I think we should protect nature and keep our environment clean for future generations.

TUẦN 7: MÙA VÀ THỜI TIẾT

Bài 13: The Four Seasons

Vietnam has two main seasons, but I like to learn about all four seasons. Spring is a beautiful time when flowers bloom and trees grow new leaves. The weather is warm and pleasant. Many people go to parks and gardens to enjoy the fresh air. Summer is hot and sunny. Children love summer because they have long holidays from school. We can go swimming, play outdoor games, and eat ice cream. Sometimes it rains heavily in summer, but the rain cools down the temperature. Autumn is cool and comfortable. Leaves change colors from green to yellow, orange, and red. It is a good time for outdoor activities like hiking and camping. Winter is cold in some countries, but not in Vietnam. In winter, people wear warm clothes and drink hot tea or coffee. They stay inside more often and spend time with family. Each season has its own beauty and special activities. I think all seasons are wonderful in their own way. Weather changes make life interesting and help us appreciate different times of the year.

Bài 14: Weather and Activities

Different types of weather make us do different activities. On sunny days, I like to play outside with my friends. We can play soccer, ride bicycles, or have picnics in the park. The sunshine makes everything bright and cheerful. My mother hangs clothes outside to dry in the sun. On rainy days, we stay inside and find indoor activities. I like to read books, draw pictures, or help my mother cook. The sound of rain on the roof is very relaxing. Sometimes we sit by the window and watch the rain fall. On windy days, children love to fly kites in open spaces. The wind carries the kites high up in the sky. It is exciting to see colorful kites dancing in the air. On cloudy days, the sky looks gray and peaceful. The temperature is usually cooler, which is nice for walking or doing outdoor work. Hot weather makes us want to stay in the shade and drink cold water. Cold weather makes us want to wear warm clothes and drink hot drinks. Each type of weather brings its own joy and activities.

I have many dreams and goals for my future. My biggest dream is to become a good English teacher. I want to help children learn English and communicate with people from other countries. I think speaking English opens many opportunities in life. To achieve this goal, I study English every day and practice speaking with my friends. I also read English books and watch English movies. Another dream is to travel around the world and see different places. I want to visit famous landmarks like the Eiffel Tower in France and the Great Wall of China. I hope to meet people from different cultures and learn about their customs. I also dream of helping poor children get education. I want to build schools and libraries in remote areas. Education is very important for everyone's future. In the near future, I want to be the best student in my class. I will study hard and participate in school activities. I also want to learn to play the piano and speak Chinese. These dreams motivate me to work hard every day and never give up.

As I finish this English learning journey, I feel excited about what comes next. These eight weeks have taught me so much. I can now read longer texts and understand new vocabulary. My pronunciation has improved, and I feel more confident speaking English. I have learned about many interesting topics like family, school, hobbies, food, animals, nature, weather, and dreams. Each story has helped me understand the world better. I have also learned that practice makes perfect. The more I read and speak, the better I become. I want to continue learning English even after this program ends. I will keep reading English books and watching English programs on TV. I also want to make friends with English-speaking people online. My teacher says that learning never stops, and I agree. There is always something new to discover and learn. I am grateful for this opportunity to improve my English skills. I hope other students will also enjoy learning English as much as I do. Thank you for joining me on this wonderful learning adventure.
